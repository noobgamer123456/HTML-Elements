Adamjee Cantonment Public School

About Us

Adamjee Cantonment Public School was established in 1960.
The school is located in Dhaka Cantonment and is known for its focus on discipline, education, and character.

Teachers

The school has 267 teachers who are highly skilled and dedicated to their students' success.
They participate in workshops and training programs to enhance their teaching skills.

Students

Our school has about 7,500 students enrolled in various classes.
They excel in both academics and extracurricular activities, achieving remarkable results every year.

Excellence

Adamjee Cantonment Public School consistently achieves 100% pass rates in board exams.
Students have received numerous awards and scholarships for their outstanding performance.
